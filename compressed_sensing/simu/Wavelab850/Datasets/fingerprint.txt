fingerprint.raw -- Gray-scale fingerprint image

Access
    Smudge = ReadImage('Fingerprint');
  
Size
    512 by 512

Gray Levels
    256
 
Source
    This is a cropped version of a fingerprint which was
    obtained by anonymous FTP from Los Alamos National Labs.
    The fingerprint was made available by Christopher Brislawn,
    and is aparently referenced in the article ``The FBI
    wavelet/scalar qunatization standard for gray-scale
    fingerprint image compression'' by J. Bradley, C. Brislawn,
    and T. Hopper.  Proc SPIE Vol. 1961, 1993.
